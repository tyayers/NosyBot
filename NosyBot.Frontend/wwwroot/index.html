What's up?

How to sort fact from fiction in the world today? By checking Facebook, Twitter or Google? No, and let's say no to algorithms deciding what we see. Just because it's possible doesn't make it a good idea. Let's get back to basics, and trust professionals to descibe the complex events around us. This web app is an attempt to showcase professional reporting from around the world. Is journalism perfect, and do we always agree with their perspectives? No, however it's a great place to start, and helps us build our own informed views of what's really up in the world.

Simply click on a country below to see the translated news from there, or scroll down to the global region consolidated view below.
